Ally - The Jira Bot

Create Jira tickets effortlessly using voice commands and messages with Ally. Our bot provides a seamless and convenient way to generate Jira tickets for your projects. Simply issue the command "create Jira ticket" to get started.

How it Works:

  1. Start by saying or typing "create Jira ticket" to initiate the ticket creation process.
  2. Ally will respond and guide you through the necessary steps to provide the ticket's summary and description.
  3. You can either speak the information or type it as a message.
  4. Once you've provided the required details, Ally will create the Jira ticket and assign a ticket number to it.
  5. Ally will confirm the successful creation of the ticket and provide you with the ticket number for reference.
